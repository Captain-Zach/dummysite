I put this page together to explain a few things about utilizing the Rules engine for setting responses based on CORS.

It's important to note that there are a few specific instances where it might look like the rules engine isn't working.

For instance, if the video is cached, it will play all cached content, even when not coming from the correct origin point.

If you notice that the video is playing where it's not supposed to be, try a hard reset by pressing Ctrl + Shift + R.
